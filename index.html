About PeaceTalk

What It Does

PeaceTalk is an AI-powered chatbot designed to detect negative emotions in your messages and respond with calming, constructive suggestions to help you communicate more peacefully.

Who It Helps

Anyone experiencing conflict, stress, or communication challenges. Whether it's workplace disputes, family disagreements, or personal frustrations, PeaceTalk provides guidance for everyone.

Technology

Built with advanced keyword detection algorithms and natural language processing, PeaceTalk uses vanilla JavaScript to analyze your messages and provide real-time peaceful communication strategies.

Contributing to Peace

By promoting mindful communication and emotional regulation, PeaceTalk helps reduce conflicts and builds stronger, more empathetic relationships in our communities.
